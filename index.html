Here at Solveance, we don't just want to share our products, we want to share experiences. The Parvum Protectors were created to prevent deaths in home robberies, especially in homes with children. The story that helped inspire us to create this product comes from the CEO themselves who lost their kids during a home robbery and now has devoted themselves to make the world a better and safer place.
